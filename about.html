Our Story & Mission

Dedicated to empowering charities and transforming communities through innovative fundraising solutions.

Our Mission

At EventForCharity, we believe that every charity deserves the tools and platform to maximize their impact. Our mission is to revolutionize digital fundraising by putting charities at the center of every interaction, ensuring their brand, values, and mission shine through in every donor experience.

We're committed to building lasting relationships between charities and their supporters, creating a sustainable ecosystem where good causes can thrive and make meaningful differences in communities worldwide.

How We Started

EventForCharity was founded in 2020 by a team of passionate technologists and charity sector veterans who recognized a critical gap in the fundraising landscape. While existing platforms focused on their own branding, charities were losing the opportunity to build meaningful relationships with their supporters.

We started with a simple question: "What if fundraising technology could amplify a charity's mission instead of overshadowing it?" This question led us to develop our charity-first approach, ensuring that every donation, every event, and every interaction strengthens the bond between charities and their communities.
